Welcome to the Tableau section of my portfolio — where data meets design, strategy, and storytelling. I specialize in uncovering trends, segmenting user behavior, and visualizing performance across time, categories, and geographies.
Projects here showcase:
- KPI development for tracking business health and operational efficiency

Whether it’s food delivery, retail, or public service, these dashboards reflect my ability to turn raw data into actionable insights — with clarity, precision, and impact.
